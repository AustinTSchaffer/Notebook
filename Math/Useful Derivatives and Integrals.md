## L'Hopital's Rule

When the limit of something is apparently $\frac{0}{0}$, go to the tiny hospital.

$$\lim_{x\rightarrow{c}}\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}=\lim_{x\rightarrow{c}}\frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)}$$
